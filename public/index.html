Mission Statement

FemAlert is a community-driven safety platform built for and by society to empower women through real-time help, safe commuting, and verified local support. Together, we ensure no one walks alone.

Key Features

🔴 SOS Button: Instantly alert contacts and authorities.
🛣️ Route Safety Checker: Choose safer routes using crime data.
📞 Fake Call Generator: Simulated calls with live location sharing.
🧍‍♀️ Overseer Network: Reach verified local volunteers nearby.
🌙 Night Commute Buddy: Commute with verified users (Premium).
📍 Periodic Check-ins: Automated safety check-ins (Premium).
